Jollof Rice

Home

Description:

A classic West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, and spices. It’s flavorful, colorful, and perfect as a main meal for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of blended tomatoes
  • 2 cups of long-grain rice
  • 1-2 cups of water or stock

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and sauté the chopped onion until soft.
  2. Add the tomato paste and blended tomatoes. Cook for 10–15 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  3. Season with curry powder, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add the rice and stir to coat it in the sauce.
  5. Pour in water or stock, cover, and cook on low heat until the rice is tender.
  6. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot.
