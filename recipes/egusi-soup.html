Egusi Soup

Home

Description:

A rich, hearty Nigerian soup made with ground melon seeds and leafy greens. It’s traditionally eaten with fufu or pounded yam.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup palm oil
  • 2 cups water or stock
  • 1 small onion, chopped/li>
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup ground egusi (melon seeds)
  • 1 teaspoon ground crayfish (optional)
  • 1-2 cups chopped spinach or ugu leaves

Steps:

  1. Heat the palm oil in a pot and sauté onions until soft.
  2. Add the ground egusi and cook for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
  3. Pour in water or stock and stir well to form a thick soup.
  4. Add the leafy greens and cook for another 5–10 minutes.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and crayfish.
  6. Serve hot with fufu, pounded yam, or any swallow of your choice.
