Palm Butter

Home

Description:

A warm and comforting sandwich made with toasted bread, cooked egg, and melted cheese. It's quick to prepare and perfect for breakfast or a light snack.

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 slice of cheeese
  • A pinch of black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or margarine

Steps:

  1. Lightly toast both slices of bread in a toaster or on a pan until golden brown.
  2. Heat a small pan over medium heat.
  3. Melt the butter.
  4. Crack the egg into the pan.
  5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  6. Cook until the white is firm (you can flip it if you want it less runny).
  7. Place the slice of cheese on one piece of toast.
  8. Add the cooked egg on top of the cheese.
  9. Cover with the second slice of bread.
